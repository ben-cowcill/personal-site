About

My coding journey started back in Summer 2018. Stuck and unfulfilled in a digital marketing role with no sense of purpose or direction, I felt a strong desire to start afresh in a new field. I had played around with some basic HTML and CSS before and so I set about looking for a way to delve into the topic further and eventually stumbled upon freeCodeCamp.


A few months later - after completing the Responsive Web Design certification I was completely hooked and ultimately decided to quit my job in order to pursue my newfound passion for coding and a career in web development.


For the past 12 months, my sole focus has been to absorb as much information as possible and to continually improve my coding skills, but soon enough, I realised that one of the most important and fundamental aspects of being a developer is something that cannot be learned in isolation - teamwork.
